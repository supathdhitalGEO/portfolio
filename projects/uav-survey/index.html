UAV Survey & Orthomosaic Processing

Project BriefUpdated May 9, 2026

Field UAV surveys used for high-resolution mapping, orthomosaic generation, and photogrammetric analysis. Work includes flight planning, ground-control collection, image processing (Pix4D/Agisoft/OpenDroneMap workflows), orthorectification, and QA for use in planning and analysis.

Detailed report: https://doi.org/10.13140/RG.2.2.35430.29767/2

What I Do

  • Design flight plans for target coverage and overlap, respecting regulatory and safety constraints.
  • Collect ground control points (GCPs) and integrate DGPS control for accurate ortho alignment.
  • Process imagery into orthomosaics and dense point clouds using standard photogrammetry toolchains.
  • Deliver orthophotos, true-ortho products, and derived indices for end users.

Why It Matters

High-resolution UAV products enable site-scale mapping where satellite imagery is too coarse. They support planning, damage assessment, and model calibration with survey-grade spatial fidelity.
